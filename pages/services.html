Services

Lifetime hosting and maintenance

When your website is finished, we deploy it, and offer a lifetime of updates and edits so that you don't have to do anything. Want to change your logo? Simple. Need to change your contact details? We can do that. Pay £40 per month, contact us for an update and we'll sort out all the technical details.

Go beyond a simple website

We can implement many different features even if it's a standard 5 page website. The upcoming premium option will include even more powerful features, specifically online transactions. This will include robust security and continuous updates to help prevent malicious attacks on your site.

This example can be seen online here. If you want to be able to manage your own blog on your website, you can sign up and log in through the admin portal. You will then be redirected to a personal builder where you can edit, create and delete blog posts.

Interative features: built-in blogs, animations and more.

Unlimited revisions

For a standard website, you can ask us to revise and change the website any number of times before and after permanent deployment. If anything doesn't feel quite right about your website, tell us. Constructive criticism is appreciated. If you decide to cancel before permanent deployment, you will be refunded your deposit money.

Fast and efficient

It only takes us on average a couple days to have everything finished. As we continue this side of business, we only keep getting faster. We are a great option if you want a polished website quick, without it being rushed, insecure and full of bugs.

In addition, we design the website to perform optimally and fast.

The process

Here is how working with us will go:

  • You come and contact us with a new website idea.
  • Make sure to send us examples of your vision, images you'd like to include, logos or anything to streamline the process.
  • We'll ask you for a £200 deposit and to sign a legal contract.
  • Once this is done, we confirm the order and get working. You'll get continuous updates throughout.
  • When the site is finished, we will send it to you for final opinions. Remember, if you don't like it, tell us!
  • Once you are pleased, you either pay the rest immediately or in monthly installments.
  • If you want us to host and maintain it, you buy the domain name and pay £75 per month.

When using images, logos, etc, make sure it is legal for them to be used!
